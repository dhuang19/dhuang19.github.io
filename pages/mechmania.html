OVERVIEW

Mechmania is a 24 hour AI hackathon held at the annual Reflections Projections conference at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

I am a developer of the hackathon.

MY CONTRIBUTION

My teammates and I were responsible for receiving streams of game data and visualizing the data in Unity3D. During MM 2018, I implemented a custom JSON parser to bridge the gap between the game engine team and the visualizer team, created a map generator for play testing, implemented the dynamic camera movement, designed the final map, and implemented some UI elements. STATISTICS

MechMania 2018 had many firsts with the use of a new client system, a language agnostic option for contestants, and live twitch streams of the matches.

While Mechmania was streaming, it was the top programming stream on twitch for its duration with a total of 585 viewers.
