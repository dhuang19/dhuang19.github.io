Archery VR: Attack of the Bunnies

VR Archery Tower Defense | October 2018 - December 2018

I am the lead programmer, game designer, and sole level designer of Archer VR.
I worked with three other students to complete this for the final project in the Virtual Reality course at my university.
I implemented everything shown in the video below. This includes a waypoint navigation system, a realistic archery mechanic, and the dynamic HUD. Through this project, I became familiar with the Oculus integration for Unity interface and learned the issues that arise with developing a virtual reality game.
